Mohammed(Md) Toufikuzzaman

State College, PA, USA

I am a Graduate Research Assistant and first-year Ph.D. student at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) of Pennsylvania State University. Previously, I was an Assistant Professor (currently on leave) at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET). I am strongly interested in Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning (ML), Bioinformatics, and Cybersecurity. I also enjoy discussing Contemporary Philosophy. I have an M.Sc. Engg. and a B.Sc. Engg. degree in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) from Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET).
